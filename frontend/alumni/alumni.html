About Alumni Cell

LNCT Group is proud to have its alumnus, as a group of 50,000+ engineers, technologists, scientists, managers, and entrepreneurs and more.

Established in 1994, Lakshmi Narain College of Technology has cultivated a vibrant and enduring relationship with its alumni community over the years. LNCT understands that the journey of education extends far beyond graduation, and thus, it is committed to nurturing lifelong connections with its former students.

The institute wholeheartedly acknowledges and takes immense pride in the pivotal role played by its esteemed alumni in our notable achievements. Our aim is to transform untapped potential into tangible, meaningful advantages for individuals, society, and our nation.
