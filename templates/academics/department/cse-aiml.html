CSE-AIML Engineering

Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the college was established in the year 1994 with an intake of 60 students. Two postgraduate courses in Computer Science and Engineering and Software Engineering were started in the year 2004 and 2007 respectively.

The Civil Engineering Department at LNCT Group was established to produce skilled engineers who are capable of tackling real-world infrastructure challenges. The department offers comprehensive theoretical and practical knowledge in structures, geotechnical, transportation, and environmental engineering. Through a curriculum aligned with the latest industry trends, the department nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and hands-on learning.

Our graduates are employed across leading infrastructure firms, government bodies, and global engineering consultancies. Students are provided with modern lab facilities, expert faculty, and opportunities to work on live projects in collaboration with top civil engineering companies.
